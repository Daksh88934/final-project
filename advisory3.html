NGOs Supporting Indian Farmers

Navdanya
Promotes biodiversity, seed sovereignty, and organic farming across India.
BAIF Development Foundation
Supports sustainable rural development, livestock care, and afforestation.
Watershed Organization Trust (WOTR)
Focuses on climate-resilient farming, water conservation, and rural empowerment.
Deccan Development Society (DDS)
Empowers women farmers and promotes ecological agriculture in Telangana.
Swayam Shikshan Prayog (SSP)
Trains women in sustainable farming and agri-entrepreneurship across Maharashtra.
SFAC (Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium)
Helps small farmers form producer organizations and access agribusiness markets.
